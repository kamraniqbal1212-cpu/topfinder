How it works

Live supplier search
Every search hits multiple UK heating suppliers and returns the public list price plus stock status. Recent searches are cached for an hour to keep things fast.
Postcode for nearest branch
Add your postcode and Top Finder finds the closest Screwfix and City Plumbing branch to you. Branch lookups are cached per postcode area for a week.
Trade pricing note
Prices shown are the supplier's public list price. If you have a trade account, your actual price at till may be lower.
